History of MPFR and Past Events

25 and 26 June 2009
CNC'2 Summer School on MPFR and MPC at LORIA, Nancy, France.
25 February 2009
Version 2.4.1 is released.
27 January 2009
Using MPFR, Keith Briggs found a new worst approximable pair (new record) in the problem of constructing an explicit pair of irrationals with sup-norm simultaneous approximation constant as large as possible.
26 January 2009
Version 2.4.0 is released.
12 September 2008
Version 2.3.2 is released.
5 March 2008
GCC 4.3.0 release: GCC now uses MPFR in its middle-end.
This has been announced on linuxfr.org.
29 January 2008
Version 2.3.1 is released.
25 and 26 October 2007
CEA-EDF-INRIA School Certified Numerical Computation (Calcul numérique certifié) at LORIA, Nancy, France.
29 August 2007
Version 2.3.0 is released.
29 November 2006
Version 2.2.1 is released.
3 and 4 October 2005
The MPFR team has won the Many Digits Friendly Competition, that took place at Radboud University, Nijmegen, The Netherlands.
